Privacy Policy for RVU Edge

Last Updated: January 31, 2026 | Effective Date: January 31, 2026

AeroEdge LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the RVU Edge mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Procedure Logs: CPT codes, wRVU values, dates, and optional notes you enter
  • User Preferences: Display name, specialty, monthly RVU goals, theme preferences
  • Subscription Information: Purchase history processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: App feature usage patterns, crash reports (anonymized)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Patient names or identifiers
  • Protected Health Information (PHI)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or personal contacts
  • Biometric data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App functionality
  • Calculate and display wRVU totals and productivity metrics
  • Store your procedure logs locally on your device
  • Process subscription payments (via App Store/Play Store)
  • Improve App performance and fix bugs
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All procedure logs, user settings, and personal data are stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite database storage. We do not operate cloud servers that store your personal data.

3.2 No Cloud Sync

Version 1.0 of RVU Edge does not include cloud synchronization. Your data remains on your device only.

3.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your locally stored data, including:

  • SQLite database encryption
  • Secure storage APIs provided by iOS and Android
  • No transmission of personal data over the internet

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Fonts

The App uses Google Fonts to display typography. Font files are downloaded at runtime from Google's servers. Google may collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser/device information

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 App Store Platforms

Subscription payments are processed through:

  • Apple App Store (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Store (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)

We do not receive or store your payment card information.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may disclose information only:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect and defend our rights or property
  • With your explicit consent

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Export

You can export all your procedure data at any time using the Export feature in the App (Settings → Export Data).

6.2 Deletion

You can delete all your data by:

  • Using the "Delete All Data" option in Settings
  • Uninstalling the App (removes all local data)

6.3 Opt-Out

You may opt out of analytics collection in Settings → Privacy.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

RVU Edge is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Portability: Export your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rvuedge.com.

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Performance of contract (providing App services)
  • Legitimate interests (improving the App)
  • Consent (optional analytics)

9. CCPA Compliance (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rvuedge.com.

Categories of Personal Information Collected (past 12 months):

Commercial information Yes No Subscription management

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored locally on your device and is not transferred internationally. Third-party services (Google Fonts, App Stores) may process data in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
